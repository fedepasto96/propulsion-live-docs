7. Key Observations & Comparison Summary

Packet Digital

  • Lowest quoted NRE at $13.5K–$18K, but certification is not yet quoted
  • Unit cost is competitive at volume ($188–$256 at 5K + $186 cells = $374–$442)
  • 15% markup if PD sources cells ($214/pack vs $186 consigned); BMS also subject to markup
  • Strong NDAA / FCC compliance (100% US manufacturing)
  • Drone/military pedigree (Anduril, Lockheed, US Navy)
  • Certification costs still pending — total non-recurring will increase significantly

Emerging Power

  • Most complete quote with full NRE + tooling + certification breakdown
  • Total non-recurring as quoted: $200,000 (NRE $5K + Tooling $110K + Cert. $85K)
  • Adjusted total excl. injection mold: $100,000 — EP is the only supplier quoting plastic injection mold tooling ($100K); this cost is common to all paths
  • Unit cost ~$590–$645 at volume includes cells
  • 2% handling charge on customer-supplied BMS
  • Full FCC compliance (NJ manufacturing)
  • Detailed cost breakdown provided at 1,000 units

Smart Battery Solutions (SBS)

  • Most comprehensive development proposal with detailed design/tooling/cert lines
  • Highest certification cost at €142,725 (~$154K) — notably CQC China at €43K
  • Competitive unit cost at 6K/yr: €506 (~$547) with SA504 cells
  • Offers three cell options (SA504, SA08, SES H10P) with different price points
  • SES H10P option dramatically cheaper at €309/unit but lower capacity (10.6 vs 11 Ah)
  • EU-based (Germany) — does not help with FCC compliance for US market

Alexander Battery Technologies (ABT)

  • Highest total non-recurring: £339K (~$431K) — dominated by £241K for 300 qualification packs
  • Lowest certification cost across all suppliers: £50,250 (~$64K) if all elected
  • Competitive unit cost at 7K/yr: £405–£449 (~$514–$570) with SA08 cells, depending on commitment length
  • Prior Amprius SA-series production experience on aviation-class programme
  • Audit-grade quality: DFMEA/PFMEA/SPC/Gauge R&R/PPAP methodology; 100% EOL on every pack
  • UK-based (Peterlee) — mitigates EU/US supply chain risk, no Far East dependency
  • Build-to-print model; all prices indicative until Cost Freeze gate
  • ISO 9001 / ISO 45001 certified facility

Overall Assessment

  • At target volume (6K–7K/yr), Packet Digital offers the lowest estimated unit cost ($374–$442 with consigned cells at $31/cell) followed by ABT 5Y (~$514), SBS (~$547) and EP ($590–$645)
  • Emerging Power has the most balanced total cost — adjusted to $100K non-recurring (excl. injection mold) + competitive unit pricing
  • ABT has the highest NRE ($431K) due to the 300-pack qualification programme, but the lowest certification cost ($64K) and competitive unit pricing with long-term commitment
  • Packet Digital’s quote is incomplete without certification — final comparison pending; unit cost advantage is significant
  • For US market / FCC, both Packet Digital and Emerging Power are suitable
  • For EU/UK supply chain, SBS (Germany) and ABT (UK) are both strong options; ABT brings prior Amprius SA-series experience and audit-grade quality methodology
  • Cell cost is the dominant factor (~64–78% of unit cost across all suppliers)
