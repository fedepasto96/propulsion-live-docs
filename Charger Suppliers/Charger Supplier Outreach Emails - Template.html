Battery Charger — Outreach Email Template

Prepared: April 2026  |  Owner: Federico Pastore  |  Companion file: Charger Supplier Scouting — Cost-Effective Asia & Global.html

Purpose. Generalised cold outreach for the suppliers listed in the cost-effective charger scouting addendum — OEM smart multi-bay chargers aligned with Flyability’s lithium smart-charger technical requirements (comms, handshake, safety standards, multi-port). Mirrors the short style of the Phase 2 contract-manufacturer template: enough to route to the right BD/product team, with full TR detail under NDA.

How to use. Replace [YELLOW] placeholders. For Mean Well–style power-stage-only vendors, add the bracketed sentence in the adaptation note so they know you may pair their module with your own control layer.

Generalised template

To: [sales / OEM / Enterprise contact or web form]
Cc: [optional: named BD or regional account]
Subject: OEM smart multi-bay Li-ion charger inquiry (Flyability drone platform)

Dear [Company] Team,

I am reaching out from Flyability SA (Switzerland), the manufacturer of the Elios family of indoor inspection drones used worldwide in the energy, mining, oil & gas, and many other sectors.

We are looking for an OEM or custom-OEM partner for a production smart charger for our next-generation onboard battery, with these key parameters:

  • Product type: multi-bay unit (target: 4 independent channels / simultaneous packs), for 6S Li-ion pouch (~26.1 V CV class; exact profile under NDA).
  • Intelligence: BMS communication (CAN or protocol TBD with you) and battery identification / handshake prior to charge; support for storage and transport-oriented modes (e.g. safe SOC windows for shipping — to be specified in our requirement set).
  • Compliance: EU placement — need IEC 62368-1-grade safety path and CE marking; other markets (e.g. FCC) TBD.
  • Volume: on the order of 1 k–10 k chargers / year over the ramp of the program (ballpark for early RFQ; may grow with product success).
  • Scope split: we can own final system architecture, protocol, and firmware where applicable; we need a partner able to deliver industrialised hardware and ODM/OEM execution to an agreed specification.

Could we schedule a 30-minute discovery call in the next 2–3 weeks to discuss feasibility, indicative unit pricing and NRE, MOQ, certification strategy, and an NDA-backed RFQ path?

If you can route this to your business-development team for the Industrial / Power & Battery Charging segment, that would be much appreciated. Happy to share detailed specifications and technical requirements under NDA on request.

Best regards,
Federico Pastore
Lead Battery Engineer, Flyability SA, Paudex

Adaptation notes

Optional second paragraph (power-stage vendors only)

After the bullet list, add this sentence if the supplier is primarily a programmable AC/DC or bidirectional power stage supplier (e.g. Mean Well BIC-class), not a turnkey smart charger OEM:

“If your role would be limited to the certified power stage and Flyability (or a nominated integrator) assumes the multi-channel control and battery handshake layer, please indicate what reference architecture you recommend on a short call.”

Subject line variants

  • Default: OEM smart multi-bay Li-ion charger inquiry (Flyability drone platform)
  • UAV/drone-native brands (Tattu/Grepow, Foxtech, Aerotate, EV-PEAK SkyRC): OEM 4-channel smart drone battery charger — Flyability RFQ
  • Tier-1 PSU / industrial (Mean Well, Phihong, Cincon, Delta, AcBel, Lite-On): Multi-channel Li-ion charger OEM — Flyability (CAN / smart battery)
EU hybrid / Germany HQ. For FRIWO, BMZ, MGM COMPRO, Schaefer, Aerotate, keep the same body but you may add one line before the sign-off: preferred manufacturing or certification footprint (e.g. Vietnam or EU) if that is a hard preference for the program.
