E4 Battery Charger — Outreach Email Drafts

Prepared: February 11, 2026
Purpose: Initial outreach to charger suppliers identified in the Charger Supplier Selection Report
Contact: Federico Pastore, Lead Battery Engineer, Flyability SA, Paudex

Charger context: The E4 charger is a smart charger, not a simple CC/CV power supply. It must communicate with the BMS, authenticate the battery, and actively discharge to 30% SOC for IATA transport compliance. Suppliers already contacted for pack assembly (Packet Digital, Tulip Tech, Apex) should receive a bundled inquiry that adds the charger scope to the existing conversation.

E4 Charger Quick Spec (for reference)

ParameterValue
Configuration6S Li-ion (silicon-anode pouch cells)
Charge voltage (CV)26.1 V ± TBD%
Charge current (CC)~13 A (1C for 13 Ah pack) — TBD
Power~340 W
Max output voltage (fault)< 31.3 V
BMS communicationSMBus, I2C, UART, or CAN (TBD, must match BMS)
Battery ID / authenticationRequired — reject unknown batteries
Active dischargeTo 30% SOC (IATA transport) and 60% SOC (storage)
SafetyIEC 62368-1 reinforced isolation
Volume~6,000–7,000 units/year at steady state
