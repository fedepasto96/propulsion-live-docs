Key Assumptions (updated from supplier quotes)

  • Cell cost: CHF 27–31/cell — based on Amprius SA-504 at ~$31/cell (consigned by Flyability). 6 cells per 6S1P pack.
  • 6S configuration: 6 cells in series per pack. Battery capacity ~11 Ah; charging voltage = 26.1 V (20.4 V nominal).
  • Overage buffer: 5% additional cells per order to cover QC rejects, incoming inspection failures, and destructive test samples.
  • BMS PCBA cost*: CHF 132–220/board at prototype quantities (was $150–$250 in v1). Includes PCB fabrication, SMT assembly, and components (TI BQ41Z90 gauge IC, MOSFETs, sense resistors, balancing circuits, connectors, passives).
  • Assembly materials: CHF 200–270/pack covers connectors, wiring harness, nickel strips/tabs, prototype enclosure, thermal interface materials, conformal coating, labels, insulation. Derived from PD’s materials-only line: CHF 184–245 at 100 units, scaled up ~10% for 75-pack quantity. This is ~72% of the total assembly cost (excl. cells & BMS), consistent across PD’s volume tiers.
  • Assembly labor: Order 1 defaults to CHF 0/pack (self-assembly by Flyability). Order 2 assumes CHF 60–80/pack at external assembler — based on PD’s labor-only line: CHF 55–73 at 100 units, scaled up ~10% for 75 packs. Total assembly (materials + labor) = CHF 260–350, consistent with PD’s CHF 238–319 at 100 units plus a small-batch premium. Full pack cost (cells + materials + labor, excl. BMS) = CHF 422–536, mid ~CHF 479 — appropriately above the CHF 461 production-volume average.
  • Pack NRE + Tooling: CHF 13,000 (PD scope: design eng. only, excl. cert. & tooling) to CHF 87,000 (SBS scope: NRE €17,325 + tooling €74,000 = €91,325 × 0.95). EP quotes $5K NRE + $110K tooling, but $100K of that is injection mold tooling common to all paths. Adjusted EP: ~CHF 13,200. PD and adjusted EP are comparable at ~CHF 13K.
  • Pack Certification: CHF 75,000 (EP: $85K × 0.88, covers CE + FCC) to CHF 136,000 (SBS: €142,725 × 0.95, covers CE + FCC/IC + Australia C-Tick + CQC China). PD’s certification is still pending quotation. Timing spans Q3 2026 through Q1 2027; some testing (UN 38.3) may overlap with prototype builds.
  • Charger NRE: CHF 28,025 (EFT adapter-only NRE) to CHF 178,640 (PD full 4-port design). Range reflects fundamentally different development scopes.
  • Charger prototype cost: CHF 1,666–2,221/unit based on PD ROM Quote #00003684 prototype pricing ($1,893–$2,524 × 0.88).
  • Charger quantity: 15–25 units across 2026. First batch (~5–10) for validation; second batch (~10–15) paired with WL prototype packs.
  • Shipping: DG (dangerous goods) surcharges apply for Li-ion cell shipments. BMS PCBA and charger shipments are standard freight.

* PCB/BMS cost estimates checked with the Electrical team. Currency: All USD values converted at 1 USD = 0.88 CHF (May 2026).

Timeline Alignment (Gantt Reference)

  • Q2-Q3 2026: BMS PCBA dev. proto batch ordered Jul, received & validated Jul–Aug. Integration testing Aug.
  • Q3 2026: Order 1 — ~50 packs. Cells production Jul–Sep, shipment Sep, pack assembly late Sep–Oct. Feeds into BMS + Pack integration (Oct–Nov).
  • Q4 2026 (Dec): Order 2 — ~70–80 packs. Placed Dec 2026, delivery expected Jan–Feb 2027 targeting 50 WL prototypes milestone.
  • Q2-Q4 2026: Battery pack NRE & tooling (design eng., fixtures, test jigs). Certification testing (UN 38.3, CE, FCC) begins Q3 and may extend into Q1 2027.
  • Q2-Q3 2026: Charger development at supplier (May–Sep). First validation samples expected mid-Q3.
  • Q3-Q4 2026: Charger prototype production (Sep–Nov). Full batch ready to pair with 20 WL prototypes in November.
