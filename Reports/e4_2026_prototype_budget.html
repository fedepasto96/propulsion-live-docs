Key Assumptions

  • Cell cost: CHF 44–132 per cell unit — based on current supplier quotations for the selected cell chemistry and expected prototype pricing volatility (converted at 1 USD ≈ 0.88 CHF).
  • 6S configuration: 6 cells in series per pack. Battery capacity ~13 Ah; charging voltage = 26.1 V.
  • Overage buffer: 5% additional cells per order to cover QC rejects, incoming inspection failures, and destructive test samples.
  • BMS PCBA cost*: CHF 132–220/board at prototype quantities. Includes PCB fabrication, SMT assembly, and components (TI BQ41Z90 gauge IC, MOSFETs, sense resistors, balancing circuits, connectors, passives). A small development batch (~15 boards) is ordered in Q3 for validation ahead of pack assembly.
  • Assembly materials: CHF 31–70/pack covers connectors (12-pin battery connector), wiring harness, nickel strips/tabs, prototype enclosure (CNC or 3D-printed housing), thermal interface materials, conformal coating, labels, and insulation.
  • Assembly labor: Order 1 defaults to CHF 0/pack (self-assembly). Order 2 assumes CHF 13–35/pack at external assembler for prototype quantities (spot welding, soldering, mechanical assembly, QC).
  • Charger: ~340 W smart charger (26.1 V × 13 A at 1C for 13 Ah pack). NRE CHF 10,560–22,000 reflects significantly more complex power electronics vs. a sub-150 W unit. Prototype sample cost CHF 264–440/unit above production pricing due to low volume and proto tooling.
  • Charger quantity: 15–25 units across 2026. First batch (~5–10) for validation; second batch (~10–15) paired with WL prototype packs.
  • Shipping: DG (dangerous goods) surcharges apply for Li-ion cell shipments ex-China. BMS PCBA and charger shipments are standard freight.

* PCB/BMS cost estimates checked with the Electrical team.

Timeline Alignment (Gantt Reference)

  • Q2-Q3 2026: BMS PCBA dev. proto batch ordered Jul, received & validated Jul–Aug. Integration testing Aug.
  • Q3 2026: Order 1 — ~50 packs. Cells production Jul–Sep, shipment Sep, pack assembly late Sep–Oct. Feeds into BMS + Pack integration (Oct–Nov).
  • Q4 2026 (Dec): Order 2 — ~70–80 packs. Placed Dec 2026, delivery expected Jan–Feb 2027 targeting 50 WL prototypes milestone.
  • Q2-Q3 2026: Charger development at supplier (May–Sep). First validation samples expected mid-Q3.
  • Q3-Q4 2026: Charger prototype production (Sep–Nov). Full batch ready to pair with 20 WL prototypes in November.
